United’s biggest team-news issue is defensive balance. They usually have enough attacking quality to create chances, but this kind of match can still get messy if transitions are not managed well.

Leeds need enough pace and aggression in the front line to turn recoveries into real counterattacks. Without that, United will have too many comfortable possession spells.

Why this game looks this way

United should have the higher ceiling in attack and the better individual quality, but Leeds can make the game messy enough to stop it becoming a clean favorite script.

Score prediction
