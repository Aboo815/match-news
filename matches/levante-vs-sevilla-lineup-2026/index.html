Analysis

Team news

Levante's selection should carry urgency, not panic. They are still fighting from a dangerous position, and the home crowd will expect them to force the issue early. The danger is obvious: push too high, leave the full-backs exposed, and Sevilla have exactly the breakaway route that can drain the stadium in a few seconds.

For Sevilla, this feels like a test of nerve as much as structure. They are above Levante, but not far enough away from trouble to play with real freedom. The away XI should be built around patience, second balls and a midfield that can keep the match from becoming a survival scrap.

Match context

Neither side arrives with the confidence of a team in full rhythm. Levante have had enough recent sparks to believe they can drag Sevilla into uncomfortable territory, while Sevilla have spent too much of the season collecting draws and narrow setbacks to feel completely secure.

The table gives the game its tension. Sevilla have the cushion, Levante have the urgency, and the January meeting between them still lingers. Levante's win in Seville gives the home side a psychological hook, even if the visitors bring more experience into the decisive moments. This is the kind of fixture that can turn quickly from a controlled away performance into a hard argument about nerve.

Prediction

One clean spell may be enough to swing this, but both teams have too much at stake for the game to stay open for long. Levante can feed off the home crowd; Sevilla have enough experience to survive the rougher periods. A low-scoring draw feels like the most honest read, not because either side lacks ambition, but because neither can afford to lose control.
