Fulham vs Aston Villa predicted lineup, team news and prediction (April 25, 2026)

Main Article

The predicted Fulham lineup should be built around control without drifting into caution. Fulham need enough authority in midfield to keep the game where they want it, but they also need runners who can turn long spells of possession into genuine threat.

Analysis

Team news

Fulham need width, but their bigger challenge is keeping the centre strong enough to stop Aston Villa turning recoveries into counters. The predicted XI has to be adventurous and sensible at once.

Aston Villa should trust compact distances and quick release points. Their shape is unlikely to be passive, but it will probably be patient.

Match context

The first goal would matter, though perhaps not in the obvious way. Either side can make the game messy enough that a lead becomes more about nerve than control.
