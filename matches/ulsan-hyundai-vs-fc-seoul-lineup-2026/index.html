FC Seoul predicted lineup

Team news

The most important issue for Ulsan is structural balance in the center. If the center-backs and holding midfield are intact, they can sustain longer attacking phases without exposing the back line after turnovers.

For FC Seoul, the key is keeping the defensive line from getting broken by repeated circulation and movement around the box. If their shape stretches too much, Ulsan will eventually find cleaner shooting positions.

Why this game looks this way

Ulsan come in as the side more likely to control the match, but control and output are not the same thing. That is the main tension here. They should own more of the ball, but if the pace stays too slow, FC Seoul can make the game flatter than expected.

This is why the risk point matters. A slow match pattern can turn apparent superiority into sterile possession, which is exactly the kind of game state that keeps a compact opponent within one moment of changing the result.

Score prediction

Ulsan should still have enough midfield quality and territorial advantage to edge the game, especially if their wide players can stretch Seoul’s defensive block consistently. The stronger read is a controlled home win, but not a runaway performance.
