Fulham predicted lineup

Fulham are more likely to defend deeper and look for moments to break into the spaces left by Liverpool’s high positioning, especially down the channels.

Predicted Fulham starting XI: Bernd Leno; Kenny Tete, Tosin Adarabioyo, Calvin Bassey, Antonee Robinson; Joao Palhinha, Andreas Pereira, Harrison Reed; Bobby Decordova-Reid, Raul Jimenez, Alex Iwobi.

Team news

Liverpool’s key team-news issue is not whether they have enough attacking quality, but whether they rotate one or two positions and still keep the same intensity from the first whistle.

Fulham need as much stability as possible in central midfield and defense. If those areas break down, Liverpool can turn the match into a wave of pressure very quickly.

Why this game looks this way

Liverpool should dominate territory, crosses, high regains and attacking volume. That alone gives them a clear edge before kickoff.

Fulham can still threaten if they survive the early pressure and find space on the break, but the game state still leans heavily toward Liverpool creating more and scoring more.

Score prediction

Liverpool should generate enough pressure and enough chances to win with margin, even if Fulham find one path to score in transition or from a second ball.
