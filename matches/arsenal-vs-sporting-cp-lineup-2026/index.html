Team news

Arsenal’s most important selection questions sit around the defensive core and midfield screen. If the center-backs and holding midfield are intact, the team can press more aggressively and keep Sporting pinned back for longer stretches.

For Sporting, the key is whether the back line can handle Arsenal’s shooting match pattern and wide rotation without getting pulled apart on switches. If they lose structure in wide-to-central transitions, the pressure will build quickly.

Why this game looks this way

Arsenal have the profile of a side that should control most of the match at home, but knockout football adds a second layer to the decision-making. Score prediction

Arsenal should create more attempts and hold more territory, especially through Rice, Odegaard and the wide front line, but Sporting have enough structure to stay in the match. The stronger read is a controlled Arsenal win rather than a fully one-sided knockout night.
